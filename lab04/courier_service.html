Courier/Delivery Service in the DMV!

This image represents the hard work and dedication that is required to start a business. The courier in the image is represents strength, doing the work that is necessary to make the business successful. This image is a reminder to me that I need to stay focused and keep pushing forward even when things get tough. The courier is also a reminder that I need to be willing to get my hands dirty and do whatever it takes to make my business successful. Overall, this image is a great representation of the entrepreneurial journey I am on and the hard work that is required to make my dreams a reality.
